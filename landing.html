light types bulb

There are various types of bulb used :

  • standard incandescent bulb: A standard incandescent bulb, also known as an incandescent lamp or Edison bulb, produces light by heating a thin wire filament until it glows. They are less energy-efficient than newer technologies like LEDs, but are known for their instant-on capability and good color rendering.

    • price: 200

  • CFL bulb: A compact fluorescent lamp (CFL), also called compact fluorescent light, energy-saving light and compact fluorescent tube.

    • price: 300

  • fluorescent lamp: fluorescent lamp, electric discharge lamp, cooler and more efficient than incandescent lamps, that produces light by the fluorescence of a phosphor coating. A fluorescent lamp consists of a glass tube filled with a mixture of argon and mercury vapour.

    • price: 500
